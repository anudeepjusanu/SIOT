About Us

We Teach. We Advise. You Choose.

Siot tribe "Smart platform for better career….”. We are one of the pioneers in industrial IoT and provide better teaching manner with different technical skills by real time application-based experiments. Siot tribe develops the self-learning kits for beginners, researchers, students, corporate, institutes and enthusiasts to help in technological research & development. We develop innovative and solitary project ideas as well as electronics-based systems & machines that can be used to automate various operations in industries. Our organisation builds some systems to automate work from various sectors including agriculture, manufacturing, defence, as well as Retail sector.

VISION

“Our main motto is to create millions of Hi-tech students world-wide and empower them with right skills and certifications for better future.”

MISSION

"Siot works to upgrade the Virtue and quality of Education of students by enlighten their skills with technology and helping students in need reach their full potential through Learning and power of IoT."
